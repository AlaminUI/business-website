Local Search Engine Optimization (Local SEO) is the process behind increasing the visibility and online traffic for local businesses.

It differs from traditional SEO since the objective is to attract web users from a specific city, state or region. For example, the goal would be to rank for the keyword “hair salon near me” or “hair salon in Atlanta” versus “hair salon”.

Critical Components of Local SEO

To see success in your Local SEO efforts – you need to nail these critical components. That can be hard to do if you don’t have experience with local SEO. That’s where our local SEO company in Charlotte comes in.

We will set up, manage, and track each of these critical SEO components for you. This way you have more time to focus on your #1 mission – running your local business.

NAP Consistency

NAP stands for Name, Address, and Phone Number. It's critical that your NAP information is consistent across all Local SEO platforms. This means that your Name, Address, and Phone Number must be the same on your website, Google My Business listing, and Local Directory listings. If your NAP information is not consistent, it will confuse search engines which will hurt your Local SEO rankings.

Local Directory Listings

Local directory listings are online directories that list local businesses in a specific category. These local directory listings help improve your local rankings by increasing your NAP visibility and building local citations.

Google My Business Listing

Your Google My Business (GMB) listing is one of the most important local SEO components. Your GMB listing includes your NAP information, business hours, business description, photos, and more. GMB listings show up in local SEO results called the Map Pack. Ranking high in these listings will lead more prospective customers to your website.

Local SEO Citations

Local SEO citations are mentions of your NAP information around the web. This could be other business websites or referral websites. It could also be major platforms like Facebook, Instagram, or LinkedIn. These local SEO citations further increase your NAP visibility and build authority for your website.

Local SEO Keywords

Local SEO keywords are keywords that include local modifiers like city, state, or zip code. These keywords help improve your rankings by increasing local relevance for your business.

Mobile-Friendly Website

A mobile-friendly website is a website that is easy to use on a mobile device. This includes both smartphones and tablets. Mobile-friendly websites help improve your local SEO rankings by driving SEO components like Time on Site, Page Load Speed. Improving these SEO components will transfer into a boost in your Google Maps rankings.

Local SEO Tracking

Local SEO tracking is the process of tracking metrics like rankings, organic local traffic, local leads, and conversions. Our local SEO company will help track and understand your SEO progress, identify local SEO opportunities, and measure your digital marketing ROI.
